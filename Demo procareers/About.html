  • ABOUT THE FOUNDER

    • ProCareers is your compass in the journey of career exploration and success. Founded by the visionary B.Tech graduate, Abhishek Mohapatra, who doubles as our founder and general manager, ProCareers was born out of a personal struggle. Abhishek faced the daunting challenge of choosing a career path that aligned with his passions, academic achievements, and family interests. Recognizing the widespread nature of this dilemma among students, he envisioned a solution that would revolutionize career guidance.
    Abhishek's own journey fuels his commitment to making ProCareers a beacon of personalized and effective career counseling. As a B.Tech graduate, he intimately understands the intricacies of academic and professional choices. His passion for empowering students led him to create ProCareers, where the aim is to provide a seamless and comprehensive approach to career selection.
    At ProCareers, we leverage cutting-edge features to help you choose a career that not only aligns with your academic achievements but also resonates with your personal aspirations and family interests. Our founder, Abhishek Mohapatra, serves as the driving force behind our commitment to revolutionizing career guidance. Join us at ProCareers and embark on a journey towards a fulfilling and successful career with confidence.
